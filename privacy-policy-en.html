Privacy Policy — CloudHanbut (구름 친구 맺기)

Effective date: 2025-09-20

1. Overview

CloudHanbut is a simple, offline puzzle game. The app does not require login or an account, does not communicate with a server during normal use, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

  • Identifiers (name, email, phone): Not collected
  • Sensitive permissions (location, contacts, photos/media, camera, microphone): Not requested/used
  • Advertising ID (IDFA) or tracking: Not used
  • Third‑party SDKs for analytics/crash reporting/ads: Not used

3. On‑Device Storage

To enable gameplay, the app stores scores and basic settings on your device only. This data is not shared with the developer or any third party and is removed when you delete the app.

  • iCloud sync: Not used
  • Network communication: No data is transmitted to a server during normal use

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for all ages and does not collect personal information from children. The app does not include external links or purchases that require parental consent mechanisms.

5. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no server‑side data to access, correct, or delete. You can remove any on‑device scores/settings by uninstalling the app.

6. Security

No personal data is stored or transmitted by the app. We nonetheless follow platform best practices to maintain app integrity and safe distribution.

7. Sharing and International Transfers

We do not share personal data with third parties and do not transfer personal data internationally.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect feature changes or applicable laws. We will post updates on this page and indicate the effective date.
